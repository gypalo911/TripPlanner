<h1>Smart Route Finding for Budget Travel</h1>

<p>TripPlanner is a lightweight iOS application that helps travelers find the cheapest route between cities. The app uses sophisticated pathfinding algorithms to analyze flight connections and determine the most economical journey, even when there are no direct flights available.</pr>

<h2>Key Features</h2>

   <div class="feature-grid">
        <div class="feature-card">
            <h3>Intelligent Route Finding</h3>
            <p>Automatically calculates the most cost-effective route between any two cities using Dijkstra's algorithm.</p>
        </div>
        <div class="feature-card">
            <h3>Interactive Map Visualization</h3>
            <p>Displays your entire journey with color-coded markers and connection lines to visualize your trip.</p>
        </div>
        <div class="feature-card">
            <h3>Smart Search</h3>
            <p>Quickly find cities with autocomplete suggestions as you type, making city selection easy and intuitive.</p>
        </div>
        <div class="feature-card">
            <h3>Clear Cost Breakdown</h3>
